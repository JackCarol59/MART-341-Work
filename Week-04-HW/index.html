Frisbee Terms

The Mark
The player holding the frisbee is 'guarded' by an opponent at a 'discs length'away (no fouling)That opponent is considered the mark
Stall Count
The Mark counts out loud to ten. If the offender has not released the disc to throw before the 'T' of ten, it is a turnover and the defense recieves the disc
Violation
When a violation is called out, play stops. An example of a violation would be a Foul, Pick or Strip
